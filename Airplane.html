Overview:

This database is designed to ensure all data is tracked in the system to avoid economic and goodwill damage. If a passenger’s baggage isn’t tracked properly, it can go to the wrong final destination and ultimately result in losing the passenger’s suitcase. Errors like this can significantly hurt an airline financially because if that bag is found, they have to pay to ship it back to the owner, and if it’s completely lost, they have to reimburse the passengers for their belongings. Additionally, they would most likely lose that customer, which would even further their profit loss. If this issue isn’t addressed, an airline could lose $6,000,000 per month and risk bankruptcy within 6 months. Our new database helps minimize these errors to ensure all data is tracked correctly in the system.
